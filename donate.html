Become a Partner in Protecting Science

Your contribution funds the tools, research, and advocacy that keep science at the center of public health policy. Every dollar helps us build a more informed, engaged democracy.

What Your Support Makes Possible

🛠️

Build New Tools

Fund development of legislative tracking tools, representative lookup systems, and civic engagement platforms that make it easy for citizens to take action.

🌎

Expand Our Reach

Help us reach more communities, train more volunteers, and bring science-based advocacy to every corner of the country.

📊

Hold Officials Accountable

Support our work monitoring legislation, analyzing policy, and ensuring that elected officials prioritize evidence over ideology.

More Than a Donation — A Partnership

When you support SAFE Action Fund, you're not just making a donation — you're becoming a partner in the fight to protect science in public policy. Our supporters are the backbone of everything we do. Together, we ensure that evidence and reason remain at the heart of the decisions that shape our communities, our health, and our future.
