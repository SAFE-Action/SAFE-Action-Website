Protecting science in public policy.

When science is ignored, public health suffers. SAFE Action Fund works to ensure that evidence — not misinformation — guides the laws that protect our communities.

Our Mission

SAFE Action Fund advances clinical integrity, scientific transparency, and evidence-based public health policy through legislative advocacy, public accountability, and civic engagement. The organization works to ensure that healthcare laws and public health decisions are guided by credible scientific evidence and serve the common good.

About SAFE Action Fund

SAFE Action Fund advocates for science-based public health policy and works to counter misinformation that threatens clinical integrity and public trust in science. By tracking legislation, engaging citizens, and holding elected officials accountable, the organization works to ensure that public health decisions reflect credible scientific evidence and protect communities.

SAFE Action Fund is organized and operated to promote social welfare by advancing clinical integrity, scientific transparency, and evidence-based public health policy. The organization engages in legislative advocacy, candidate accountability, and public education to help ensure that healthcare laws protect the common good and general welfare of the community.

SAFE Action Fund is a 501(c)(4) social welfare organization. Contributions are not tax deductible.

What We Do

📋

Legislative Monitoring

SAFE Action Fund tracks state and federal legislation that affects public health, scientific research, and healthcare policy.

🔬

Policy Analysis

We analyze legislative proposals and public health policy to identify where scientific evidence is being ignored, distorted, or misrepresented.

🗳️

Civic Engagement

We provide tools that allow citizens to contact elected officials and advocate for evidence-based public health decisions.

⚖️

Accountability

We monitor how policymakers respond to public health legislation and promote transparency in government decision-making.

Why This Matters

Public health policies shape the safety, well-being, and resilience of communities across the country. Decisions made by lawmakers affect everything from vaccine policy and medical research to healthcare access and the strength of our public health systems.

When misinformation or political pressure overrides credible scientific evidence, the consequences can affect entire communities. SAFE Action Fund exists to ensure that science remains central to public policy discussions and that decision-makers remain accountable to the public good.
